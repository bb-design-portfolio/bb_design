Brooke Burnside was born in Nassau, Bahamas. A student of notable Bahamian artists, such as Jessica Colebrooke, Sue Bennett-Williams, and Celeste Mitchell, she was introduced to the possibilities of art through a wide variety of media. She studied Film at Vassar College and received her Master’s degree in Media, Culture, and Communication from New York University. After gaining work experience at international creative institutions like Dia Art Foundation and BOMB Magazine, Burnside attended an intensive summer course in Architecture at Harvard University’s Graduate School of Design where her love of research, writing, and creating came together. In the Fall of 2013, Burnside returned to the Bahamas and similarly returned to the practice of making art. She now lives in Austin, Texas and is pursuing a Master's in Architecture when her hands are not covered in clay.
